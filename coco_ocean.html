Welcome to Coco Ocean

It's not every day you take a vacation, so why not splurge on a little luxury? The Coco Ocean Resort & Spa (One Bamboo Drive) and the Kairaba Beach Hotel (390 Senegambia Road) are a couple of the top upmarket properties here. On the other hand, you wouldn't go on vacation to stay in your room all week. If you're after a more economical option, try the Palm Beach Hotel (Kotu Stream Road) or the Palma Rima Hotel (350 Serrekunda).
